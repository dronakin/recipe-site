Simple Indian Curry

Home

Ingredients

  • 28oz Can of Crushed Tomatoes.
  • 1 Diced Shallot (Half an onion-ish)
  • 1 Cup cauliflower - Could use broccoli
  • 1/2 A zucchini
  • 1/3 of a bell pepper
  • 1 - 12oz Heavy Cream
  • 4 TBSP Butter
  • 5 Cloves Garlic
  • 2.5 TBSP Garam Marsala
  • 1 TBSP Kashmiri Chili Powder
  • 1 TSP Crushed Red Chili Pepper
  • 2 TSP Tumeric
  • 2 TSP Cumin
  • 1 TSP Brown Sugar
  • 2 TBSP Ginger
  • 2 TSP Coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. If you’re adding meat, cook the meat in batches to sear it off and cook it. Add to sauce once sauce is complete. I usually cook the meat once I’ve started the sauce.
  2. Open can of crushed tomatoes and put in pot with seasonings and veggies.
  3. Cook on a simmer (low-medium) for about 25 minutes. Stirring occasionally (I do it about every 8-10 minutes, as it’s simmering and won’t burn)
  4. Add in the heavy cream and cook for about 5-10 minutes, until the flavors mix. This is the stage to adjust any seasonings as needed for taste.
  5. Remove the pot from heat and stir in butter until melted.
